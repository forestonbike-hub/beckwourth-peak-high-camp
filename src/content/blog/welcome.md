We're building something here at Beckwourth Peak High Camp, and this blog is where we'll share the stories behind it.

Expect trail guides for the Lost Sierra's best gravel and mountain bike routes, trip reports from guest adventures, and honest accounts of what it's like to run an off-grid glamping property at 5,300 feet in the Northern Sierra.

We'll cover everything from the best time to ride Mills Peak to where to grab a burger after a long day on the bike. If you're planning a trip to the Lost Sierra, this is where you'll find the inside info.
